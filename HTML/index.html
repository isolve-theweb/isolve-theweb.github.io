Who is GOD to me? Ans: The Great I Am (Ever present to help, make ways and bless me)

The Waymaker is watching and is with me. I don't see a problem, I see a promise. " If you want to get to the place where you can serve GOD greatly; you have to start by making decisions, thinking thoughts that place GOD'S honour first". "You must start by honouring GOD everyday." " If you cannot do great things, do small things in a great way". Napoleon Hill. Proverbs 3:25-26 is my promise and GOD is faithful. Therefore, I have no fear of sudden disaster or of the ruin that overtakes the wicked, for the Lord is my confidence and will keep my foot from being snared".
